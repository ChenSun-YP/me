About

I am a PhD student in Biomedical Engineering at USC. In the Neural Modeling and Interface Lab, I work on experimental neural recordings—spike sorting, signal analysis, and stochastic-gradient-based embedding models for high-dimensional neural data. My background includes building scalable Python services in industry; I use Python and PyTorch for modeling, signal processing, and neural decoding.
